Khatu Shyam Ji Temple is one of the most sacred and renowned pilgrimage sites in Rajasthan, dedicated to Lord Shyam, believed to be the manifestation of Barbarik, the valiant grandson of Bhima from the Mahabharata. The temple, located in the peaceful town of Khatu in Sikar district, attracts millions of devotees every year who come to seek blessings and offer heartfelt prayers. Its white marble structure, adorned with intricate carvings and a golden crown on the idol of Shyam Baba, radiates divine charm and serenity. The temple is especially famous for the grand celebrations during Phalgun Mela and the continuous chanting of “Shyam Naam” that fills the air with devotion and spiritual energy.
